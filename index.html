About Us

We started planning College Scholar on August 14, 2017. It was quite a struggle for the past two weeks because we had just finished the Girls Who Code (GWC) curriculum and given a task for our final project on the topic of college. When we started the project, everything was going smoothly; however, we faced many challenges when we were trying to publish our website with Heroku. As a result, we had to restart our homepage from scratch. It took some time to rebuild what we have lost but we finished in time (thankfully). We hope to continue improving our website for your convience. We hope you found this site helpful.
